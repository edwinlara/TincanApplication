Put money away for your next trip, gadget, or rainy day. Tincan, a smart piggy bank that makes saving simple.

SYNC ACCOUNTS

Tincan is an app that helps you save money intelligently by storing funds in something we call a "can". To use Tincan, you must sync your bank accounts. It's quick, simple, and secure.

CHOOSE OR CREATE

Choose to save funds in standard cans like "Vacation" and "Education" or create your own like "Lollapalooza" and "New iPhone".

SELECT SAVINGS

Decide how and when you'd like to save. Withdraw funds into your savings cans on paydays, when shopping, through the help of our algorithms, or do it manually. Additionally, you can setup scheduled withdraws e.g. daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, or yearly.

FRIENDS & FAMILY

Tincan allows you to save collectively making it easy for you to save for a road trip with your friends or for a new kitchen with your partner.

CHIP IN

Need help saving for rent or textbooks? Family and friends can help by depositing funds directly into your preferred savings cans.

OPTIMIZE

Tincan can help predict when financial troubles are ahead and give you advice on how to save more by looking at your financial data.
